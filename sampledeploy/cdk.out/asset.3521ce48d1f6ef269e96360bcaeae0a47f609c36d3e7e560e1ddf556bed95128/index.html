Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about East Medical's AI Scribe for medical documentation.

How accurate is East Medical&apos;s AI Scribe for medical terminology?

East Medical&apos;s AI Scribe is trained on extensive medical datasets and achieves over 95% accuracy for medical terminology, including medications, procedures, and diagnoses. Our system continuously improves with feedback from healthcare professionals.

Is East Medical&apos;s AI Scribe HIPAA compliant?

Yes, East Medical&apos;s AI Scribe is fully HIPAA compliant. We implement end-to-end encryption, secure data storage, and strict access controls. We provide a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) for all healthcare customers.

How does East Medical&apos;s AI Scribe integrate with my EHR?

East Medical&apos;s AI Scribe easily copy and pastes the generated notes into your EHR.

Can East Medical&apos;s AI Scribe handle different medical specialties?

Yes, East Medical&apos;s AI Scribe is designed to work across specialties including family medicine, internal medicine, pediatrics, psychiatry, and more. We offer specialty-specific templates and terminology recognition.

How long does it take to generate a SOAP note?

Most SOAP notes are generated within 1-2 minutes after the patient encounter ends. The exact time depends on the length and complexity of the visit.

Do you offer training for my practice?

Yes, all plans include initial training and onboarding. Enterprise plans include dedicated training sessions for your entire staff and ongoing support.
